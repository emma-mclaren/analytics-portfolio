Since there is a very low variance for both the Pct_Native_American (0.247437) and Pct_Native_Hawaiian_Pacific_Isla (0.079760), in comparison to variables like Pct_First_Language_Not_English (404.383426) and Pct_White (877.534691), then these smaller variance variables may not appear to have as much an influence in the cluster analysis even though they could have more importance. It is necessary to normalize these so these variables can be compared more accurately in the scope of determining clusters fairly.
